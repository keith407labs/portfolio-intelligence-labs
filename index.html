The Economics of Accuracy

The Cost of Being Wrong

A single "ghost claim" can wipe out the margin of ten performing ones. We calculate the mathematical distance between the seller's tape and the trustee's reality.

Catching the "Margin Killers"

  • ✓ Ch 13 → 7 Conversions: We flag cases with "Means Test" failures or bad-faith filings before they exit the payment pool.
  • ✓ Erratic Payers: If a debtor misses 2 of the last 4 payments, your NPV shouldn't be 100%. We apply a volatility decay.
  • ✓ Cramdowns & Plan Amendments: We track motions to amend plans that lower the "pot" for unsecured creditors mid-stream.
