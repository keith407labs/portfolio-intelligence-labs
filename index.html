Methodology

From Bid Tape to Alpha

Uncover the delta between the confirmed plan and live Trustee disbursements to prevent overbidding.

Reconstruct Payment Reality

We pull full disbursement histories from trustee ledgers—not confirmed plans. Every payment, every claim, weighted by recency so you value the current trajectory, not stale plan promises.

trustee reconciliation decay weighting

Model the Claim Waterfall

We project future payments through the Chapter 13 priority structure: administrative claims first, then secured, then unsecured. Your claim's position determines its recovery—we show you exactly where it sits.

priority allocation secured vs unsecured

Deliver Risk-Adjusted NPV

We discount projected cashflows and apply dismissal hazard curves. You get base case NPV plus downside scenarios—so you can set a bid with confidence and defend it to your IC.
